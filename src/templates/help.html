Timeline Tailor Help

To produce a timeline, follow these steps:

1. Copy text from another document or website. We recommend using Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C over the entire source document (references and footnotes included - Timeline Builder can parse them out). We presently support a minimum of 500 characters and a maximum of 500,000 characters (~100,000 words)

2. Paste the text into the main text box on the home page.

3. Click "Get Timeline" and wait until processing completes. This may take a minute or so, especially if the text is large.

Here are some examples we have processed already:

A history of The Beatles (from The Beatles Wikipedia).

The famous Australian legal case of Chamberlain v The Queen (from The Original High Court Judgment).
